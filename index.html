      • Kro's Nest

        Kro's Nest
        Ex-Pats obsessed with finding pizza that’s authentically Big Apple need look no further than Kro's Nest--the keyword being big. This New York Style pizzeria is for serious eaters only, with the large measuring in at a gargantuan 23 inches! Each pizza creation is lovingly made by taking soft, hand-tossed dough and brilliantly layering quality ingredients onto each perfectly packed pie. Satisfy any kind of craving with killer combinations like the classic “Kitchen Sink” or more outrageous options like “Chicken Alfredo” or “Shrimp on the Barbie.” Creative twists on party favorites like wings, dips, nachos and fries make Kro’s Nest a must-have for your next big bash!

      • Union Bar & Grill
        American
        Union Bar & Grill
        Union Bar and Grill promises fresh, modern American cuisine in a more affordable, unpretentious light than some of its Beijing competition. Anything beef here is sure to be a treat with premium imported marbled steak (and since it’s farm-raised in Mongolia under expert American agricultural supervision, it’s an ethical satisfaction, too). There are plenty of quality options for everyone, from light and crisp salads for the health-conscious to hearty pasta, fish, and sandwich varieties for those looking to get the most bang for their buck. Looking to indulge a little? We highly recommend the Molten Chocolate Cake and one of the imported all-American micro-brews.
